Technical support
   

At JSCAPE we strive to provide useful and timely technical support. If you have any technical support issues regarding Secure FTP Factory please e-mail your support questions to support@jscape.com

When requesting e-mail support please be sure to include any source code which may help us in answering your question. JSCAPE will make every effort to answer your technical support questions in a timely manner. Most technical support questions can be resolved within 12-24 hours.
